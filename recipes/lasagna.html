World's Best Lasagna

Description

This is the best lasagna in the world, right now I want to share it to you how to make it.

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spice and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
